⚔ HOW TO PLAY — DESCENT INTO ETERNITY

OBJECTIVE

You are an adventurer descending into an ancient evil. Fight through zones, defeat bosses, level up, and gear up until you face the final darkness.

COMBAT — TURN BASED

  • Combat is turn-based — you act first, then the enemy acts.
  • Each round you have: 1 Action, 1 Bonus Action, 1 Reaction.
  • Click skills on the LEFT PANEL to use them.
  • Actions are powerful skills. Bonus Actions are quick moves. Reactions trigger automatically or on-click when the enemy attacks.
  • When you're done, click END TURN — the enemy then takes their turn.
  • You don't have to use all your actions — sometimes saving a Reaction is wise!

ZONE PROGRESSION

  • The bar at the top shows your kill progress through the zone.
  • Kill enough enemies and the BOSS spawns.
  • Defeat the boss to unlock the next zone.

CAMPFIRE

  • After defeating a boss, you reach a Campfire.
  • REST — Fully restore HP and spell slots.
  • CRAFT — Use materials dropped by enemies to make potions.
  • SHOP — Spend gold on gear from a traveling merchant.
  • EVENT — A random encounter with choices that affect your run.

LEVELING UP

  • You gain XP from kills. Level up to get more HP, ATK, and DEF.
  • Every 3 levels you pick a passive Talent that permanently changes how you play.
  • At Level 3 you unlock your Subclass — a specialization that grants unique bonuses.

INVENTORY & GEAR

  • Enemies drop gear. Click items in your inventory to equip, use, or sell them.
  • The right panel shows your equipped gear and inventory.
  • Upgrades in the right panel can be bought with gold for permanent stat boosts.

PAUSE & SPEED

  • Press PAUSE at any time to freeze the game.
  • The enemy's turn resolves on a short timer — if you don't react, it happens automatically.
