⚔ HOW TO PLAY — DESCENT INTO ETERNITY

⚔ THE BASICS

You are an adventurer descending into ancient evil. Each fight is turn-based — you act first, then the enemy responds. Survive long enough to slay the zone boss, then push deeper.

YOUR ACTION ECONOMY

  • Action — Your main move. Big damage, powerful spells, or key abilities. You get 1 per turn.
  • Bonus Action — A quick secondary move. Heals, buffs, or fast strikes. Also 1 per turn.
  • Reaction — Triggered on the enemy's turn. Counters, shields, or instant effects. It resets each round — don't let it go to waste.
  • Skills are shown in the LEFT PANEL. Each is labelled [A], [B], or [R] for Action, Bonus, or Reaction.
  • When finished, click END TURN. You don't have to use every action — saving a Reaction can swing a fight.

SKILL COSTS & COOLDOWNS

  • Skills cost your class resource (Momentum, Spell Slots, Combo Points, etc.) shown at the top of the skill panel.
  • A skill with a number on it is on cooldown — that's how many turns remain before you can use it again.
  • Grayed-out skills mean you lack the resource, the action type was already used, or it's on cooldown.

ULTIMATE ABILITIES

  • Every class has a powerful Ultimate skill — usable once per rest. It resets at the Campfire.
  • Ultimates are your biggest tools: massive AoE damage, a free extra turn, full self-heals, or game-changing field effects.
  • Save your Ultimate for the boss — or for the moment a regular fight starts to go wrong.

COMBAT STATES & CONDITIONS

  • Advantage — Roll twice, take the higher result.
  • Vulnerable — Target takes increased damage from all sources.
  • Burning / Poisoned / Bleeding — Damage-over-time that ticks each round.
  • Stunned — The afflicted creature loses their next turn.
  • Restrained — Target is locked in place; attacks against them gain Advantage.
  • Frightened — Target deals reduced damage while the fear persists.
  • Weakened — Target's defenses are reduced.
  • Shield — Absorbs incoming damage before HP is reduced.
  • Rage (Barbarian) — Reduces incoming physical damage and boosts melee hits while active.
  • Status icons appear beneath HP bars. Read the combat log for details.

CRITICAL HITS

  • A natural 20 (or expanded crit range with Champion/Evoker) deals double dice damage.
  • Some skills trigger bonus effects on crits — Surge Strike grants a free Bonus Action; Paladin crits are the ideal moment to land a Divine Smite.

ZONE PROGRESSION

  • The kill progress bar at the top fills as you defeat enemies.
  • Once full, the BOSS spawns. Bosses are tougher, have special abilities, and may enter a stronger Phase 2.
  • Defeat the boss to reach the Campfire — rest, craft, shop, or face a random event before the next zone.
  • After Zone V's boss, your run ends and you return to Elderfen in victory.

THE 8 CLASSES

Each class plays differently. Your class determines your primary resource, skill set, stat scaling, subclass at Level 3, and Ultimate ability.

⚔️
FIGHTER
Resource: Momentum
Subclass: Champion — expanded crit range, Surge Strike unlocked
Surge Strike is your finisher — and it gives a free Bonus Action on a crit. Use Second Wind between fights to conserve potions. Ultimate: Action Surge — a full extra turn at reduced costs.
🔮
WIZARD
Resource: Spell Slots
Subclass: Evoker — Evocation spells deal maximum damage
Spell slots are finite until you rest. Never waste a Level 3 slot on trash. Cantrips (Fire Bolt) are always free. Ultimate: Time Stop — 3 free actions while the enemy is frozen.
🗡️
ROGUE
Resource: Combo Points
Subclass: Thief — Pickpocket lets you steal gold, potions, or materials
Build Combo Points with basic attacks, spend on Sneak Attack. Always fight with Advantage. Ultimate: Vanishing Act — go untargetable for 2 rounds; next attack auto-crits.
🛡️
PALADIN
Resource: Holy Power
Subclass: Oath of Devotion — Sacred Weapon and Holy Nimbus
Divine Smite on crits — always. Lay on Hands is your emergency heal pool. Divine Shield can fully negate a hit. Ultimate: Divine Intervention — massive AoE radiant damage and a full self-heal.
🏹
RANGER
Resource: Focus
Subclass: Beast Master — companion attacks and Pack Hunt strike together
Apply Hunter's Mark turn 1, every fight — it multiplies your output. Beast's Aid intercepts hits for you. Ultimate: Hail of Arrows — AoE volley that Marks and Restrains all enemies.
💢
BARBARIAN
Resource: Rage
Subclass: Berserker — Frenzy auto-attacks on every turn while raging
Rage before the first hit lands — it's both your offense and defense. Reckless Attack hits hard but costs HP; save it for when you're healthy. Ultimate: World Breaker — shockwave that deals double damage to conditioned enemies.
✝️
CLERIC
Resource: Devotion
Subclass: Life Domain — Preserve Life heals for massive amounts
Channel Divinity often beats spending Devotion charges. Sacred Flame is your free damage cantrip. Spiritual Weapon deals passive damage each turn. Ultimate: Miracle — full heal yourself and blast all enemies with radiant damage.
🌿
DRUID
Resource: Nature's Charge
Subclass: Circle of the Moon — Elemental Form and Elemental Strike
Wild Shape gives you a second HP bar. Elemental Form upgrades this further with fire damage and condition immunity. Pop a form the moment your real HP drops low. Ultimate: Primal Avatar — massive HP surge and nature burst hitting all enemies.

LEVELING UP

  • Earn XP by killing enemies. Your level is shown in the top bar.
  • Each level grants more Max HP, ATK, and DEF.
  • At Level 3 you choose your Subclass — this permanently specialises your playstyle and unlocks a new skill.
  • Every 3 levels you pick a Talent — a passive that changes how your class works. Choose carefully; Talents are permanent for the run.

CAMPFIRE (BETWEEN BOSSES)

  • After each boss you stop at a Campfire before the next zone.
  • REST — Restores HP (60% of max by default) and refills all class resources and Ultimate uses.
  • CRAFT — Use materials dropped by enemies to brew potions and consumables.
  • SHOP — Buy equipment from a travelling merchant. Better items appear in deeper zones.
  • EVENT — A random encounter with real consequences. Read all options carefully before choosing.

✨ GRACES

  • Graces are powerful passive bonuses earned by completing achievements and challenges.
  • Equip them at the Grace Vault in Elderfen (beside the dungeon gate). Each grace slot can hold one grace.
  • Graces persist across runs on the same save slot — they are your long-term power progression.
  • Higher-rarity graces have stronger effects. Improve your drop chances with the Grace Affinity upgrade at the Proving Grounds.

RUN MODIFIERS

  • Before descending you can add Run Modifiers to change the rules of the run.
  • Modifiers increase difficulty (enemies deal more damage, have more HP, crits are more dangerous) but reward you with bonus gold, better drops, or more XP.
  • Some modifiers are unlocked by earning achievements. Stack them for a harder — and more rewarding — run.

SAVING YOUR GAME

  • The game auto-saves after every fight and boss kill.
  • You can also manually save from the Pause menu.
  • Multiple save slots are available from the title screen — each slot tracks its own graces, upgrades, and achievements independently.

ELDERFEN — YOUR BASE

Between dungeon runs you visit Elderfen. Walk around, talk to NPCs, and use each building for its services. Press E to interact.

LOCATIONS

  • 🍺 The Rusted Flagon (Tavern) — Rook, ex-soldier, is always at the bar. Buy him a drink (5g) to hear a zone rumor with intel on upcoming enemies.
  • ⛪ Shrine of Seraphine (Temple) — Receive a free class-specific Blessing once per visit. She'll also pray for a free HP restore if you're injured.
  • ⚒ Aldric's Forge — Sharpen your weapon (+2 ATK, 15g) once per visit. Sell unwanted materials here for gold.
  • 📚 Malachar's Study — Donate gold to unlock access to Rare and then Legendary items in Mirela's shop. This investment is permanent for the run.
  • 🏛 The Proving Grounds — Spend gold on Permanent Upgrades that persist across every run on your save slot: more HP, ATK, DEF, XP, gold, and class masteries.
  • 🏨 The Broken Compass (Inn) — Rosalind runs the inn. Visit for conversation; the inn reflects the mood of the town as the dungeon deepens.
  • 🛒 Market District — Browse stalls for goods and rumours. Vendors' stock and dialogue shift as the zone threat grows.
  • 🛍 Mirela's Goods — Buy weapons, armor, gloves, rings, amulets, and potions. Higher shop tiers (unlocked via Malachar) stock rarer gear.
  • ✨ Grace Vault — Equip Graces here. Graces are passive bonuses earned through achievements that carry forward across runs.
  • 📦 The Stash — Personal storage. Park items you don't need mid-run and retrieve them before the next descent via the Prepare screen.
  • 📋 Notice Board — Zone threat reports, scout rumors, and class-specific tips. Check it before leaving.
  • ⚰ Graveyard — A record of every hero you've lost. Visit to see their stories; a ghost of your last fallen hero may appear inside.

TOWN TIPS

  • Temple blessings and Forge sharpening reset each time you return to town.
  • Invest in Malachar early — better shop tiers compound across the entire run.
  • Proving Grounds upgrades are cross-run. Spend gold there even on losing runs.
  • NPC dialogue changes based on your class and how deep into the dungeon you've gone — talk to everyone.

EQUIPMENT SLOTS

  • You have slots for Weapon, Offhand, Armor, Helmet, Gloves, Boots, Ring 1, Ring 2, and Amulet.
  • Your equipped items are shown in the RIGHT PANEL during combat.
  • Click an inventory item to see its stats, then click EQUIP to wear it. The displaced item returns to inventory.

ITEM RARITIES

  • Common — Basic stats. Good early on.
  • Uncommon — Better stats, sometimes a minor passive.
  • Rare — Strong stats with a notable special effect. Unlock access via Malachar's Study.
  • Epic — Powerful stats with strong passives. Found in deeper zones and from special sources.
  • Legendary — Unique effects that fundamentally change how your build works. Build around these.

SET BONUSES

  • Certain items belong to Sets. Equip 2 or more pieces of the same set to activate bonus effects.
  • Set bonus descriptions appear in the item tooltip. Full sets grant the most powerful bonuses.

POTIONS & CONSUMABLES

  • Potions appear in inventory. Click to use during or outside combat.
  • Health Potions restore HP. Other potions grant temporary buffs like ATK boost, shields, or condition cures.
  • Craft more potions at the Campfire using materials dropped by enemies.

PERMANENT UPGRADES (THE PROVING GROUNDS)

  • Visit The Proving Grounds in Elderfen to spend gold on upgrades that persist across every run on your save slot.
  • Universal upgrades: Vitality (+HP), Might (+ATK), Fortitude (+DEF), Fortune (starting gold), Alchemist (starting potions), Scholar (+XP%), Precision (+crit range), Restful (better campfire healing), Haggler (shop discounts), Grace Affinity (higher grace drop rarity).
  • Each class also has a Class Mastery upgrade with a flat stat bonus tailored to that class.

💡 GENERAL TIPS

  • Don't hoard potions. If you're below 50% HP going into a boss, drink now — dead heroes carry nothing.
  • Read skill descriptions carefully. Many have hidden synergies (e.g. Paladin crit + Divine Smite = massive burst).
  • The Pause menu is available any time mid-combat. Use it to read the combat log or plan your next move.
  • If an enemy is nearly dead, burn a cooldown to finish it before it acts — saving resources for a corpse is wasteful.
  • Check the Notice Board in Elderfen before every run — it posts zone-specific tips for your class.
  • Invest in Malachar's Study donations early. Better shop tiers compound across the whole run.
  • Reactions are a free action in your opponent's phase — always have one ready when the enemy has a big hit coming.
  • Spend gold at the Proving Grounds even on losing runs. Those upgrades carry over to your next attempt.
  • Before descending, use the Prepare screen to pull items from your Stash into your starting loadout.
  • Visit the Grace Vault each return — newly earned graces won't help you until they're equipped.

⚠ CLASS-SPECIFIC TIPS

  • Fighter — Save Action Surge for a turn when you're about to be overwhelmed. Second Wind between fights conserves potions.
  • Wizard — Spell slot economy is everything. Fire Bolt is free — use it as filler and save Level 3 slots for bosses.
  • Rogue — Always fight with Advantage. Vanishing Act gives you a free auto-crit setup — use it to burst a boss.
  • Paladin — Divine Smite on every crit, always. Keep Holy Power for Smite; use Lay on Hands as your emergency heal.
  • Ranger — Hunter's Mark is your engine. Apply it turn 1, every fight, no exceptions. Pack Hunt doubles your Mark turns.
  • Barbarian — Rage before the first hit lands. Rage is defense as much as offense. Reckless Attack is powerful but costly — only use it when healthy.
  • Cleric — Channel Divinity often beats spending Devotion charges. Spiritual Weapon passively deals damage every turn once summoned.
  • Druid — Wild Shape and Elemental Form are second lives. Use them the moment real HP starts dropping, not at zero.
